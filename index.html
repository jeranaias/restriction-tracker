Install for Offline Use

This tool works offline! After your first visit, you can use it without internet.

Desktop (Chrome/Edge):

Click the install icon in the address bar, or use the "Install App" button above when available.

iPhone/iPad:

Tap Share → "Add to Home Screen"

Android:

Tap the menu (3 dots) → "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App"

Once installed, the app icon appears on your home screen and works without internet.

About This Tool

Restriction Tracker is a free, open-source tool for tracking muster sign-ins for personnel on restriction or EPD. All data stays on your device - nothing is sent to any server.

For UNCLASSIFIED use only. Never enter classified information.
